Maui Fire Research: UH Manoa & UH Maui College students as well as graduate students from US universities and expert university and national laboratory teams working together in the research funded by Rapid Response Research (RAPID) grants from the National Science Foundation (NSF).

Oceanit: Climate Change Toolkit at Paia Mantokuji: The urgency of this initiative cannot be overstated. The shoreline fronting the historic Buddhist temple at Mantokuji Mission is facing severe erosion, with its beach vanishing at an alarming rate. Our team, including fellow Maui college students, is dedicated to finding sustainable, ecosystem-based solutions to counteract this predicament.
